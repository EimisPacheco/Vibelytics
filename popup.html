Vibelytics

Feel the vibe. Follow the insights.

Turn your YouTube comment section into a powerful insight engine. See audience sentiment, top comments, and business opportunities.
1. Navigate to any YouTube video
2. Look for the control panel on the right side of the page
3. Click "Analyze Comments" to generate insights
4. Use "Export Comments" to download as CSV file
Note: The extension only works on YouTube video pages. Make sure to allow sufficient time for all comments to load before exporting.
